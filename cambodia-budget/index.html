29 Oct 2014 • on Cambodia • by Alan Richards

Cambodia Budget

We had a much higher budget set for Cambodia than Thailand or Vietnam, mostly because getting passes and transport for Angkor can be expensive. We actually found lots of Cambodia to be slightly cheaper than Thailand, although Phnom Penh hotels were expensive.

For Cambodia our budget was the very scientific number of CAD$90.20. We came up with these numbers from various online blogs, Nomadic Matt, Lonely Planet, etc.

In the end, we were on budget pretty much exactly for our first week in Siem Reap, but spent much less the following week in Phnom Penh. Overall our average was CAD$78.63 (US$70.36). Pretty good, and it makes up a little for our excesses in Thailand!

Again, the images above are from the spending tracking app on the iPhone, Trail Wallet, written by the great folks who write Never Ending Voyage. We especially like the quote in the middle which changes each time you open the app and depends whether you're over or under for the day.

Alan Richards
