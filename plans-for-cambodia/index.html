22 Sep 2014 • on Cambodia • by Alan Richards

Plans for Cambodia...

Since we're now on our last couple days in Thailand, we've started looking ahead to Cambodia. At the moment we've decided to cross at the usual land crossing of Aranyaprahet/Poipet, and continue into Siem Reap.

We'll spend probably a week here and visit Angkor Wat and some other nearby temples.

After this we're not quite so sure. We have two weeks total planned for Cambodia. Probably we'll continue to Phnom Penh and check it out, but it sounds like it's not the best place to visit. From my readings, I've heard good things about Kampot or Kep. Any ideas?
