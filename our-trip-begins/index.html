26 Aug 2014 • by Alan Richards

Our trip begins

Our trip begins with a late night departure from YVR. Thanks to rides from Auntie Sharon and Heather, we arrived at the airport around midnight ready to start our adventure. We flew Philippine Airlines to Manila arriving an hour early, but with nothing much to do in Manila Airport, and no money to buy anything! Apparently even in an international terminal, everything is cash only except the duty free name brand purses. The flight was pretty good, and the 13.5 hours went by pretty fast as we slept for 75% of the flight. A pretty good service as we had 3 decent enough meals, ample legroom, and a really good seatback entertainment system. I almost made it through two movies, and now I don't know the last 15 minutes of the new 300 sequel.

We continued in a similarly sized but less equipped plane to Bangkok. No entertainment system but we did get another meal. Once in Bangkok we found our way out through immigration (luckily neither the airline nor immigration ended up asking about onward transportation out of Thailand). Bangkok sensibly has a train link between the airport and downtown, and so it was a quick 25 minutes, plus a short cab ride to our hostel.

Alan Richards
